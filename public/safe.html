Security at Monotide

Asset Protection

We prioritize the security of your assets, implementing various measures to secure them against potential threats. Crypto assets are stored in highly secure cold storage facilities, subject to external audit. Your assets remain exclusively yours.

Regulation, Registration, licences

The Monotide Group meticulously adheres to American laws and regulations. Our operations are conducted in compliance with various EMI registrations, as well as licenses for NMITI , E-Money, and VFD II across all our key markets.

User Data Protection

Monotide exclusively processes your personal information for purposes sanctioned by the CCPA. We abstain from utilizing personal data for automated decision-making or profiling, and we have implemented several security measures to ensure the secure storage and processing of your data.

Financial Transparency

We also boast one of the most stringent regulatory frameworks. Our commitment to doing things properly, rather than taking shortcuts, is evident in our complete transparency, acquisition of licenses that subject us to oversight from multiple financial market authorities, and regular validation of our funds and business practices by external entities.

Secure and regulated

We have attained the SOC 2 Type 1 report. This is a significant achievement that demonstrates our ongoing commitment to the highest security and compliance standards.

Keeping your account secure

Password management

User passwords are stored using the most trusted and modern hashing algorithms. Nobody can read or decrypt them, including us.

Session management

Monotide allows users to see which browsers and devices they are logged in from to control their account access.

MFA

If activated by the user (highly recommended by Monotide) time-based one-time passwords and multi-factor authentication to defend against security threats and additional 2FA in case of user password compromise.

Account Monitoring

Important notifications to the user to ensure overview of user account activity.

KYC

Robust KYC onboarding and verification procedures, and fully compliant with American regulation.
