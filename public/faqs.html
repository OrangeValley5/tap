Frequently asked questions(Faqs)

This is an overview on the most frequently asked questions by our users and and the best and detailed answers to your questions.

Is this website an ICO
No. This is not an Initial Coin Offering. We believe that ICO's should be approached with caution as the majority of "Alt coins" do not offer any benefits to more established crypto currencies such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc. Monotide is a managed cryptocurrency trading platform with user friendly interface and attractive offer.
What markets do you trade
Tradeable Coins: Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum, Bitcoin Cash and XRP.
What is the risk for my investment
There is no risk whatsoever. Just invest and enjoy the financial freedom..
How can i access my account ?
If you are a registered user of Monotide, please enter your email and password in the appropriate fields at the top of the website and click the "Login to Account" button. You will be redirected to your account automatically as soon as you have done the above.
Is there any limit for an investment account ? How can i keep my accout safe
We take all security measures to protect your account and keep it safe from third parties intrusion.
What is the process of investing ?
To make investments you should register with Monotide, create an account and then you can make your deposit. All the investments are made in your personal account after login
I forgot my password
Go to Password Reminder section, enter your registration e-mail address and follow the instructions.
How can i create an account
It's not difficult at all. Click on the following button, fill in the registration form and create a new account.
Can i create more than one account ?
No. you are allowed to create only one account. For special situations please contact our Customer Support.
